Heres some facts about pinto beans

Pinto beans are free of gluten and cholesterol and are rich in protein, fiber and folate.

Pinto beans also have essential nutrients such as iron, magnesium, thiamin, calcium and zinc. They are low in fat and sodium when they are prepared without any salt and additives.
